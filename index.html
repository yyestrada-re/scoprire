COVID-19 has changed everything...

According to the Chicago Tribune, "more than 4,400 businesses have permenently or temporarily closed since March." That is a problem. A BIG problem.

In addition to providing vital jobs to the local community, small businesses also play a huge role in stimulating the local economy in low-income neighborhoods.

And we're here to fix that.

We're Scoprire and we help educate local communities on proper eating habits and offer affordable, convenient meal plans to help promote healthy lifestyles in lower income households. We do so by providing a free platform where users can explore and post their own healthy recipes that have many different price ranges, according to which ingredients are needed.
